AI Evaluation Criteria

We evaluate AI systems based on the following key criteria to ensure their reliability, fairness, and compliance:

Factual Correctness

Ensuring the AI provides accurate and verifiable information.

Adherence to Specification

Verifying that the AI meets the defined requirements and performs as intended.

Language and Grammar

Assessing the quality and coherence of the AI's language output.

Legal Compliance

Ensuring the AI operates within legal and regulatory boundaries.

Language Adequacy

Evaluating the AI's ability to communicate effectively and appropriately.

Technical Environment

Assessing the AI's compatibility and performance within different technical environments.

Privacy and Data Security

Ensuring the AI handles user data responsibly and securely.
